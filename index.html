    Be Respectful and Patient

    • Be kind, patient, and respectful when interacting with volunteers.
    • Do not harass volunteers if you are uncomfortable with their assistance.
    • Politely ask for help when needed.

    Provide Complete Information

    • Include attachments such as images, videos, or log files when applicable.
    • Provide detailed descriptions: browser used, extensions, steps to reproduce the issue, device specifications.

    Backend Development Queries

    • Do not ask about backend development (e.g., APIs, hosts).
    • Such questions will not be entertained by developers.
    Important Note: These guidelines should be followed when asking for support. Non-compliance may result in having @No Support or higher punishments.

    Professional Conduct for Helpers

    Maintain Professionalism

    • Treat users like clients and provide a user-friendly experience.
    • Use clear, concise, and grammatically correct language.
    • Avoid hasty responses; research or consult other helpers if unsure.
    • Stay knowledgeable about the website and common issues.
    • Do not disclose sensitive information or discuss internal affairs outside staff channels.
    • Remain calm and composed, especially with difficult users.

    Handling Troublesome Users

    • Apply @No Support role via command: ?nosupport <UserID>
    • Inform an Admin or Head Staff after applying the role.

    Role Outside Support Channels

    • Help prevent rule-breaking conversations and issue verbal warnings.
    • Report spam or major rule breaks to @Moderator.
    • Participate actively in community chats and uphold server rules.
    • Build a respectful persona; you may be promoted to @Moderator over time.
    • Do not interfere in moderation cases handled by @Moderators.

    Avoiding Spoon-feeding

    Encourage independent problem-solving by guiding users through steps. Avoid providing direct solutions; instead, explain how to access features or settings.

    Incorrect Approach

    Member: Can't navigate to my settings. Can you help set it up?

    Helper: Sure, Here's a direct link & my preferences, you can copy it.

    Correct Approach

    Member: Can't navigate to my settings. Can you help set it up?

    Helper: You may click on "this" feature to access the menu and customize it to your preference.

    Frequently Asked Questions (Helper Edition)

    Note: Ensure users disable adblockers when troubleshooting, as they may break site features.

    1. Missing Subtitles

    Cause: Adblocker blocking subtitles.

    Solution: Disable adblocker and refresh the page. Check for multiple English subtitle options.

    2. Subtitles Settings Reset

    Cause: Blocked cookies or cookie blockers (e.g., Brave Shields).

    Solution: Allow cookies and disable cookie blockers.

    3. Continue Watching Not Working

    Cause: Blocked cookies or cookie blockers.

    Solution: Allow cookies and disable cookie blockers/Brave Shields.

    4. No Verification Emails

    Commonly Affected: iCloudMail and HotMail users.

    Solution: Forward the user's email ID to the designated admin for manual verification.

    5. MAL Export Not Working

    Cause: Incompatible .XML file from the website.

    Solution: Inform users that the issue will not be fixed soon.

    6. Fullscreen Not Working on Chrome (iOS)

    Cause: JWPlayer bug.

    Solution: Use a different browser (e.g., Safari, Firefox, Opera).

    7. Black Screen/Infinite Loading

    Solution: Clear the browser cache (via browser settings, not device settings for Android users).

    8. "Not Allowed to Do This" Error During Registration

    Cause: One account per IP address allowed within 24 hours.

    Solution: Wait 24 hours before attempting to create another account.
